# Practiq Marketing Workspace

This folder holds Practiq marketing strategy, positioning, copy, outreach, research, demo scripts, and experiment tracking.

Practiq positioning:

> Practiq is a calmer, smarter practice system for independent healthcare practitioners.

Core message:

> Less admin. Better notes. Smarter workflows.

Primary audiences:

- Acupuncture
- Chiropractic
- Massage therapy
- Physiotherapy
- Wellness / integrative care
- Solo practitioners
- Small clinics

Current public CTAs:

- Explore Demo
- Join Early Access

Tone principles:

- Calm, practical, honest, and practitioner-centered.
- Avoid hype and exaggerated AI claims.
- Avoid sounding like a corporate SaaS machine.
- Avoid over-apologizing for early access.
- Emphasize active development and practitioner-shaped product decisions.

Folder guide:

- `strategy/`: positioning, audience, messaging, and marketing roadmap.
- `copy/`: website copy, founder story, pricing language, and future copy ideas.
- `facebook-posts/`: discovery questions, soft-launch posts, and group posting rules.
- `outreach/`: interview scripts, early-access emails, and follow-ups.
- `research/`: competitor notes, billing notes, and practitioner pain points.
- `demo-scripts/`: short demo flows for live calls and recordings.
- `experiments/`: response tracking and marketing experiment logs.
