# Insurance Billing Notes

Practiq positioning:

- Practiq is not billing-first software.
- Practiq supports billing-related workflows without becoming a bloated hospital EHR.
- SOAP / Insurance Mode supports structured documentation when needed.
- Future billing packet direction should help practitioners gather documentation and visit details clearly.

Research questions:

- Which disciplines most often need SOAP notes?
- What information do practitioners need in a billing packet?
- What documentation is required by payers vs internal policy?
- Where do practitioners lose time preparing claims or supporting documentation?
- What can Practiq support without becoming a full clearinghouse?

Potential feature direction:

- SOAP / Insurance Mode.
- Completed Visit Note status.
- Billing packet summary.
- Service fees and checkout records.
- Exportable patient/visit documentation.
- Practitioner-controlled AI documentation checks.

Messaging caution:

- Do not overpromise insurance reimbursement.
- Do not imply legal or billing compliance guarantees.
- Keep wording practical: billing support, billing-related workflows, billing packet direction.
