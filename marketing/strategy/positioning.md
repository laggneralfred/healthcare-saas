# Positioning

Practiq is a calmer, smarter practice system for independent healthcare practitioners.

It is built for acupuncture, chiropractic, massage therapy, physiotherapy, wellness, and integrative care practices that need modern software without hospital-style complexity.

Core message:

> Less admin. Better notes. Smarter workflows.

Positioning statement:

> Practiq helps solo practitioners and small clinics manage visits, notes, reminders, patient communication, intake, checkout, billing support, import, and export in one calmer workflow, with practical AI support where it actually helps.

What Practiq is:

- A practitioner-first practice system.
- A calmer alternative to bloated EHRs and generic booking tools.
- A clinical workflow product shaped around daily practice realities.
- A place to manage patient records, scheduling, Visit Notes, communication, forms, checkout, and practice data.
- An early-access product where practitioners have a real voice.

What Practiq is not:

- A hospital EHR.
- Billing-first software.
- A generic appointment booking app.
- An AI gimmick.
- A faceless enterprise SaaS platform.

Founder positioning:

> Practiq is built by a former practicing acupuncturist and longtime software developer.

Preferred founder line:

> Practiq is built by someone who understands both the treatment room and the codebase.

Avoid:

- Retired acupuncturist.
- Beta and not perfect.
- Unfinished.
- Experimental.
- Hobby project.
- Side project.
