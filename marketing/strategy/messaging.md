# Messaging

## Core Message

Less admin. Better notes. Smarter workflows.

## Primary Value Proposition

Practiq helps independent healthcare practitioners manage daily practice work in one calmer system: patient records, scheduling, Visit Notes, SOAP documentation, reminders, patient communication, intake forms, checkout, billing support, import, and export.

## Short Pitch

Practiq is a calmer, smarter practice system for independent healthcare practitioners. It helps small practices manage visits, notes, reminders, communication, intake, checkout, and data workflows with practical AI support where it actually helps.

## Founder Pitch

Practiq is built by a former practicing acupuncturist and longtime software developer. It is built by someone who understands both the treatment room and the codebase.

## AI Message

AI assists. You decide.

Use AI to help draft, organize, import, and communicate. Practiq should never imply that AI replaces practitioner judgment or automatically finalizes clinical documentation.

## Early Access Message

Practiq is in active development with early users who have a real voice in what gets built next.

Preferred phrases:

- Early access.
- Practitioner-shaped.
- Active development.
- Explore the demo with sample data.
- Help shape the roadmap.

Avoid:

- Beta.
- Not perfect.
- Unfinished.
- Experimental.
- Please forgive rough edges.
