# Marketing Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation

Goals:

- Clarify positioning.
- Make homepage demo-first and early-access oriented.
- Create reusable founder story.
- Build basic outreach assets.
- Gather practitioner feedback.

Success signals:

- Practitioners understand who Practiq is for.
- Demo clicks increase.
- Early access signups come from the right disciplines.
- Feedback identifies repeated pain points.

## Phase 2: Discovery and Community

Channels:

- Practitioner Facebook groups.
- Direct practitioner interviews.
- Existing professional networks.
- Small clinic owners.

Activities:

- Ask discovery questions before pitching.
- Run short interview calls.
- Share calm product updates.
- Track objections and repeated phrases.

## Phase 3: Demo and Early Access

Activities:

- Offer sample-data demo.
- Invite useful-fit practitioners into early access.
- Run two-minute and five-minute demos.
- Collect post-demo feedback.

## Phase 4: Message Refinement

Use feedback to refine:

- Homepage headline.
- Discipline-specific pages.
- AI language.
- Pricing confidence.
- Demo flow.
- Onboarding sequence.
