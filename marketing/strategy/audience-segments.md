# Audience Segments

## Solo Acupuncturists

Likely needs:

- Natural Visit Notes.
- Point and technique documentation.
- Intake and consent forms.
- Simple scheduling.
- Checkout and payments.
- Optional herb and product inventory.

Message:

> A calmer system for acupuncture notes, visits, intake, checkout, and patient communication.

## Chiropractors

Likely needs:

- SOAP or structured documentation.
- Treatment plan tracking.
- Follow-up reminders.
- Patient communication.
- Scheduling and checkout.

Message:

> Keep visits, notes, reminders, and checkout organized without adding EHR noise.

## Massage Therapists

Likely needs:

- Simple treatment notes.
- Client history and areas treated.
- Intake and consent.
- Follow-up care instructions.
- Scheduling and payments.

Message:

> Manage clients, treatment notes, forms, and follow-up communication in one calm workspace.

## Physiotherapy / Physical Therapy

Likely needs:

- SOAP notes.
- Goals and progress tracking.
- Exercise/home-care communication.
- Insurance-ready documentation where needed.

Message:

> Support structured documentation and follow-up workflows without turning every session into paperwork.

## Wellness / Integrative Care

Likely needs:

- Flexible notes.
- Multiple service types.
- Patient records.
- Reminders and communication.
- Import/export ownership.

Message:

> A flexible practice system for integrative care workflows that do not fit generic medical software.

## Small Clinics

Likely needs:

- Multi-practitioner scheduling.
- Shared patient records.
- Consistent documentation.
- Intake, checkout, payments, and data portability.

Message:

> A modern practice system for small clinics that need structure without hospital-scale complexity.
