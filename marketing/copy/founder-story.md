# Founder Story

Short version:

> Practiq is built by someone who understands both the treatment room and the codebase.

Standard version:

> Practiq is built by a former practicing acupuncturist and longtime software developer. After years of seeing how clinical software often gets in the way of the practitioner&rsquo;s thinking, I wanted to create something calmer: a system that supports notes, scheduling, reminders, communication, intake, billing support, and daily practice workflow without turning every visit into data entry.

Early-access close:

> Practiq is in active development, and early users have a real voice in what gets built next.

Useful angles:

- Built from lived clinical workflow experience.
- Designed by someone who can connect practitioner pain points directly to product decisions.
- Not a faceless SaaS product.
- Not hospital-first.
- Not billing-first.

Avoid:

- Retired acupuncturist.
- Hobby project.
- Experimental.
- Unfinished.
- Apologizing for early access.
