# Pricing Language

Current pricing:

- Solo: $49/month for one practitioner.
- Clinic: $99/month for small clinics with up to 5 practitioners.
- Growing Practice: $199/month for larger or expanding practices.
- Optional Herb & Product Inventory: $19/month.

Current heading:

> Simple early-access pricing.

Supporting copy:

> Practiq is currently available in early access for practitioners who want to help shape the product.

Small note:

> Explore the demo first. No credit card required.

Tone goals:

- Clear and confident.
- Early-access appropriate.
- Not pushy.
- Not discount-driven.

Avoid:

- Limited-time pressure.
- Enterprise-style pricing language.
- Overpromising maturity.
- Free trial as the main CTA.
