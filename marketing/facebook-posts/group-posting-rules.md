# Group Posting Rules

Before posting:

- Read group rules.
- Avoid direct promotion if not allowed.
- Ask discovery questions first.
- Do not spam multiple groups with identical language.
- Do not claim broad market proof without evidence.
- Be transparent that Practiq is in early access.

Preferred post style:

- Practitioner question first.
- Short context.
- Founder perspective if relevant.
- Invite feedback.
- Demo link only where allowed.

Avoid:

- Aggressive sales language.
- Discount pushes.
- AI hype.
- Criticizing specific competitors by name.
- Posting screenshots with fake patient data that looks real.

Track:

- Group name.
- Post URL.
- Date.
- Topic.
- Reactions.
- Comments.
- Direct messages.
- Useful objections or phrases.
