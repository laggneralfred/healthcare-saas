# Founding Practitioner Review Program - Short Questionnaire

Thank you for reviewing Practiq. This short version should take about 5-10 minutes.

Completing this questionnaire does not require subscribing. Eligible practitioners who complete the review and choose to subscribe receive 50% off their first 3 paid months. This is not a lifetime discount.

## About Your Practice

1. What kind of practice do you run?
2. Are you solo, or do you work with other practitioners or staff?
3. What system do you currently use for scheduling, forms, notes, and patient communication?

## Setup

4. Was the initial setup checklist clear?
5. Which setup step felt easiest?
6. Which setup step felt confusing, tedious, or incomplete?

## Website Links and Appointment Requests

7. Would you put Practiq's public links on your website?
8. Did the new patient request and existing patient access flow make sense?
9. Did the appointment request workflow feel clear as a request, not an automatic booking?

## Scheduling

10. Did practitioner working hours and treatment compatibility make sense?
11. Were deterministic suggested openings helpful?
12. What would you need before trusting Practiq for day-to-day scheduling?

## Forms and Notes

13. Did the online forms workflow match how you collect patient information?
14. Did the visit note experience feel natural for your practice style?
15. What documentation fields or templates were missing?

## AI and Follow-Up

16. Did the AI assistance feel useful, unnecessary, or concerning?
17. Did the follow-up workflow help you identify patients who may need contact?
18. What patient communication safeguards would you expect?

## Pricing and Subscription

19. Does the pricing feel reasonable for your practice?
20. What would make you hesitate to subscribe?
21. What would make Practiq worth paying for?

## Final Feedback

22. What is the strongest part of Practiq right now?
23. What is the biggest blocker?
24. Would you consider subscribing after the trial? Why or why not?
