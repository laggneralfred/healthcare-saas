# Founding Practitioner Review Program - Discount Summary

This is a plain-language summary for the Practiq Founding Practitioner Review Program. It is not a legal terms document.

## Offer

Eligible practitioners who complete the Practiq review and choose to subscribe receive 50% off their first 3 paid months.

## Important Clarifications

- The review discount is not a lifetime discount.
- Completing the review does not require subscribing.
- A practitioner can complete the review and decide not to continue with Practiq.
- The discount applies only if the practitioner chooses to start a paid subscription after the review.
- The discount is intended as a thank-you for practical product feedback during the founding practitioner review period.

## What Counts As Completing The Review

Practiq may treat the review as complete when the practitioner has:

- Set up or meaningfully explored a trial practice.
- Reviewed core workflows such as setup, website links, appointment requests, forms, notes, AI assistance, follow-up, and scheduling.
- Completed the short questionnaire or provided equivalent written feedback.

The full questionnaire is optional and intended for practitioners who want to give deeper feedback.

## Program Framing

The Founding Practitioner Review Program is designed to learn from real practitioners before broader marketing and sales efforts.

The goal is not to pressure practitioners into subscribing. The goal is to understand whether Practiq is clear, useful, trustworthy, and priced appropriately for solo and small practices.

## Internal Notes

- Do not describe the offer as a permanent or lifetime discount.
- Do not imply that a subscription is required to participate.
- Do not promise custom development in exchange for participation.
