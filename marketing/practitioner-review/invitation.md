# Founding Practitioner Review Program

Practiq is inviting a small group of independent health practitioners to review the product during its early rollout.

This is for practitioners who are willing to set up a real or realistic trial practice, try the core workflows, and give direct feedback on what feels useful, confusing, missing, or not worth paying for yet.

## What You Will Review

- Practice setup and first-run checklist
- Public website links for new patient requests and existing patient access
- Appointment request workflow from patient to staff
- Practitioner and treatment type setup
- Working hours and scheduling suggestions
- Online forms for new and existing patients
- Visit notes and clinical documentation
- AI assistance for notes and follow-up messages
- Follow-up workflow for patients who may need care
- Pricing, subscription concerns, and adoption blockers

## Review Offer

Eligible practitioners who complete the review and choose to subscribe receive 50% off their first 3 paid months.

This is not a lifetime discount. Completing the review does not require subscribing. The discount is simply a thank-you for giving practical feedback during the founding practitioner review period.

## What We Ask From You

- Try Practiq with your own practice workflow in mind.
- Complete the short questionnaire.
- Optionally complete the full questionnaire if you have more detailed feedback.
- Be candid about what would stop you from using or recommending Practiq.

## Who This Is For

Practiq is currently focused on solo and small health practices, including acupuncture, massage therapy, chiropractic, physiotherapy, and related wellness clinics.

The most useful reviewers are practitioners who understand the day-to-day work of scheduling, forms, notes, payments, and patient follow-up.

## How To Participate

1. Start or request access to a Practiq trial.
2. Set up your practice profile, practitioner, treatment types, working hours, and website links.
3. Try the patient-facing and staff-facing workflows.
4. Complete the questionnaire.
5. If you decide Practiq is a fit, subscribe and request the review discount for your first 3 paid months.
