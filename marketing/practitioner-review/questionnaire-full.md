# Founding Practitioner Review Program - Full Questionnaire

Thank you for taking the time to give detailed feedback on Practiq.

This questionnaire is meant to learn what Practiq must do to be genuinely useful for small healthcare and wellness practices. Please answer only the sections that are relevant to your workflow.

Completing this questionnaire does not require subscribing. Eligible practitioners who complete the review and choose to subscribe receive 50% off their first 3 paid months. This is not a lifetime discount.

## 1. Practice Background

1. What discipline or services do you provide?
2. Are you solo, part of a group practice, or managing staff?
3. How many appointments do you typically handle per week?
4. Do patients book online today, request appointments, call/email, or use a mix?
5. What tools do you currently use for scheduling, forms, notes, billing, and follow-up?
6. What do you like and dislike about your current system?

## 2. Trial Setup Experience

1. Was it clear what you needed to configure before using Practiq?
2. Did the setup checklist include the right items?
3. Was the practice profile and slug setup understandable?
4. Was practitioner setup clear?
5. Was treatment or appointment type setup clear?
6. Was practitioner-to-treatment compatibility understandable?
7. Was working-hours setup clear enough to support scheduling?
8. What setup step would you want explained better?
9. What setup step should be automated, simplified, or moved later?

## 3. Website Links

1. Would you place the New Patient Request link on your website?
2. Would you place the Existing Patient Access link on your website?
3. Would you place the Request Appointment link on your website?
4. Did the copy/paste HTML snippets seem useful?
5. Did the explanations make it clear what each link does?
6. What wording would you use on your own website buttons?
7. What concerns would you have before advertising these links publicly?

## 4. Appointment Requests and Booking Workflow

1. Did the appointment request workflow match how you want patients to contact you?
2. Was it clear that Practiq creates requests, not automatic bookings?
3. Did structured treatment selection feel useful?
4. Did practitioner preference or "No preference" make sense?
5. Should patients be able to add more detail before submitting a request?
6. What should staff see first when handling a request?
7. Did calendar-first scheduling for preferred practitioners make sense?
8. Did form-first scheduling with suggestions for no-preference requests make sense?
9. What would you need before allowing patients to self-book, if anything?

## 5. Scheduling Rules and Suggestions

1. Did practitioner working hours behave the way you expected?
2. Did unavailable time blocks make sense?
3. Did treatment compatibility with practitioners make sense?
4. Were deterministic available slot suggestions helpful?
5. Did the suggestions feel trustworthy?
6. What information was missing when choosing a time?
7. Would you want room, equipment, or resource scheduling later?
8. What scheduling mistakes would be unacceptable in your practice?

## 6. Online Forms

1. Did the new patient interest and form flow match your intake process?
2. Did the existing patient form workflow make sense?
3. Was it clear that forms do not automatically overwrite patient demographics?
4. What intake questions or form types were missing?
5. Do you need consent forms, health history, SOAP intake, insurance information, or discipline-specific forms?
6. How should staff review submitted forms?
7. What would make online forms safe enough for your practice?

## 7. Visit Notes and Documentation

1. Did the visit note screen fit how you document care?
2. Do you prefer simple notes, SOAP-style notes, or another structure?
3. Were the templates appropriate for your discipline?
4. What fields or sections were missing?
5. Did the note flow work on a phone or tablet?
6. How important is dictation or mobile note-taking?
7. What would make documentation faster without feeling unsafe?

## 8. AI Assistance

1. Did AI assistance feel useful in visit notes?
2. Did AI assistance feel useful for follow-up messages?
3. Did the AI suggestions feel too generic, too clinical, too wordy, or helpful?
4. What should AI never do in your practice?
5. Was it clear that staff remain responsible for reviewing and saving content?
6. Would AI support increase or decrease your trust in the product?
7. What AI feature would be most valuable if implemented carefully?

## 9. Follow-Up Workflow

1. Did the Follow-Up page identify useful patient follow-up opportunities?
2. Were the care status labels understandable?
3. Did invite-back drafts feel appropriate?
4. Would you use follow-up reminders in real practice?
5. What patients should Practiq never automatically contact?
6. What follow-up controls or audit trail would you expect?

## 10. Pricing and Subscription Concerns

1. Does the current pricing make sense for your practice size?
2. Which plan would you consider?
3. What monthly price would feel clearly worthwhile?
4. What price would make you hesitate?
5. Which missing feature would prevent you from subscribing?
6. Which existing feature most supports subscribing?
7. Would the 50% off first 3 paid months review discount help you try Practiq longer?
8. Since the review offer is not a lifetime discount, what would need to be true by month 4 for you to continue?

## 11. Trust, Safety, and Adoption

1. What would you need to trust Practiq with real patient workflows?
2. What data privacy or security questions would you ask before subscribing?
3. What would you want explained during onboarding?
4. Would you want help importing existing patients?
5. Would you want a guided setup call?
6. What would make you recommend Practiq to another practitioner?

## 12. Final Assessment

1. What is the strongest part of Practiq right now?
2. What is the weakest part?
3. What felt unfinished?
4. What felt better than your current tools?
5. What should be built next?
6. Would you subscribe after the trial? Why or why not?
7. May we follow up with questions about your answers?
